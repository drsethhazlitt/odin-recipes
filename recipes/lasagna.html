Ingredients

the sawce

  • 1 medium onion finely diced
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, finely diced
  • 1 lb of ground round
  • 1/8 tsp spicy red chilli flakes optional
  • 1/8 tsp ground fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup beef-style broth
  • 1 28 oz can of San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 300 g package of lasagna sheets
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 stalks of fresh basil
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 cup shredded vegan mozzarella
  • 1-2 tbsp granulated sugar

tofu ricotta

  • 1 350g block of firm/extra firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp fortified nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp white miso
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt & pepper to taste

Directions

    tomato sauce

  1. In a large saucepan on medium heat, saute onions in 2 tbsp of olive oil until translucent
  2. Add ground round and break up any chunks
  3. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cook for 2-3 mins
  4. Add tomato paste, fennel, oregano, and balsamic vinegar and mix for 1 minute
  5. Add broth and let simmer for 3 minutes
  6. Add tomatoes and break them up in the pot, let cook for 45 minutes on medium-low
  7. Blend all ricotta ingredients until grainy, but not smooth.
  8. Soak pasta sheets in cold water for 5 - 10 mins, making sure they don't stick together
