Ingredients

the sawce

  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, finely diced
  • 1 lb of ground round
  • 1/8 tsp spicy red chilli flakes optional
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 28 oz can of San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 300 g package of lasagna sheets
  • salt
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 1/8 tsp ground fennel seeds
  • 2 stalks of fresh basil
  • 1 cup shredded vegan mozzarella
  • 1-2 tbsp granulated sugar

tofu ricotta

  • 1 350g block of firm/extra firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp fortified nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp white miso
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt & pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Blend all ricotta ingredients until grainy, but not smooth.
  2. In a large saucepan on medium heat, saute onions until translucent
  3. add garlic and red pepper flakes
  5. Soak pasta sheets in cold water for 5 - 10 mins, making sure they don't stick together
