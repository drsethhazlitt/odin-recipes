Ingredients

  • 8 medium-size peeled russet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tsp salt + 1 tsp for boiling water

Directions

  1. Add some salt to a 6 quart pot of cold tap water - about 1 tsp
  2. Cut the potatoes into 1/4 pieces and add to the water; put the pot on to boil on high heat
  3. Once brought to a boil, turn down the heat to medium-high and take the lid off to avoid boiling over
  4. Let cook for 15 - 20 minutes until potato is easy to pierce with a knife
